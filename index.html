List of Jollof rice ingredients to get from the store!

Below are the necessary Ingredients to get from the store should you want to prepare a very good Nigerian Jollof rice!
  • Local Rice
  • Tomatoes(Fresh not can preferably)
  • Green Vegetables
  • Pepper, Onion, Vegetable oil&Meat(or chicken)
  • Spices
    • Maggi
    • Salt
    • Curry and Thyme
    • Grinded Creyfish
    • Carrot and Green Bean
    • Jollof rice spice

Step by Step procedure on your cooking Journey!

  1. Place the pot on fire with a measured amount of water in it all ready to boil
  2. Perboil the rice by putting the already measured rice into the pot and letting it boil with the water a bit
  3. Remove the rice into a separate bowl to wash gently and empty the perboiled water from the pot and add Vegetable oil
  4. Wash the rice and shifter again to properly remove the starch element from the body
  5. Add your grinded/blended tomatoes,pepper&onion, measured salt and maggi, already cutted vegetable, grinded creyfish, curry and thyme to the already frying oil and wait to properly fry
  6. Add a measured water into the pot and wait for it to boil a bit
  7. Add your rice and let it cook
  8. Whilest it's almost done, add your already fried meat or chicken and you already neatly cutted carrot and green bean into the food and let it cook a bit more
  9. Remove the pot from fire and dish out your Meal!
  • Lo and behold you are now officially a chef, Congratulations!
This list does not guarantee you a perfect run at the end of your cooking but just like they say, practice makes perfect! with time you should be a professional.
